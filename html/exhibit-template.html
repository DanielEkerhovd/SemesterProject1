EXPLORE OUR ECOSYSTEM

Welcome to the "Explore Our Ecosystem" exhibit, a captivating journey through the intricate web of life that sustains our planet. In this interactive science museum experience, you'll delve into the beauty and complexity of ecosystems, discovering the delicate balance that exists between organisms and their environment.

FOOD WEB

Witness the interdependence of life as you manipulate a touch-screen food web simulation. Connect the arrows between organisms to see how energy flows through various trophic levels, highlighting the vital role each species plays in maintaining the ecosystem's stability.

ADAPTATION STATION

Engage with hands-on exhibits that showcase the incredible ways in which organisms adapt to survive in their environments. From camouflage to mimicry, you'll discover the fascinating strategies that animals and plants employ to thrive amidst challenges.

CLIMATE CHANGE THEATER

Step into an interactive theatre where you can experience the impacts of climate change on different habitats. Use a control panel to adjust temperature, rainfall, and other factors, and observe how these changes affect the plants and animals within the ecosystems.

BUILD YOUR OWN ECOSYSTEM

Release your inner ecologist by designing your own miniature ecosystem. Select plants, animals, and environmental factors to create a balanced, self-sustaining world within a sealed terrarium. Witness firsthand the challenges and rewards of ecosystem management.
